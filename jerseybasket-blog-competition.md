# Just 4 Shopping Receipts Could Land You a £10 Voucher

We're giving away a **£10 gift voucher** — and it couldn't be easier to win.

During June, JerseyBasket is running its first ever **Price Hunt Competition**. Every time you spot a grocery price in a Jersey store that isn't on the app yet, snap a photo of your receipt and send it in. The person who submits the most verified prices by 30 June wins the voucher — redeemable at the store of their choice.

Last week's leader got there with just four receipts. Four.

## How it works

1. Do your normal shop
2. Spot something on your receipt that's not on JerseyBasket yet
3. Submit it through the app — takes about 30 seconds
4. We verify it, add it, and count it toward your total

That's it. No special trips, no extra effort. Just your regular weekly shop.

## Why we're doing this

JerseyBasket launched less than two weeks ago and already has over 700 active users. But with five stores and hundreds of products, there are still gaps — and nobody knows Jersey's shelves better than the people who shop them every week.

Every price you submit makes the app more useful for every family on the island.
