**Prepared by Eamonn O'Shea | June 2026**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

JerseyBasket.je is Jersey's first free online grocery price comparison platform. It allows Jersey residents to compare the price of grocery items across all five major supermarkets — CI Co-op, Morrisons, M&S Food, Waitrose, and Iceland — helping families find the cheapest weekly shop without leaving their home.

Launched in late May 2026 with zero marketing spend, JerseyBasket reached 1,000 active users within weeks through word of mouth alone. The platform currently lists 549 products across 15 categories and is growing daily through a community-driven price submission model.

Revenue is generated through banner advertising within the app, with a tiered package structure ranging from £99 to £999 per month. The platform is built on a lean, low-cost technical stack with minimal ongoing running costs.

### What We Do
JerseyBasket.je is a free, browser-based web app — no download required. Users can:
- Search and browse 549+ grocery products
- Build a virtual basket
- Instantly see which of Jersey's 5 supermarkets is cheapest for their whole shop
- Save favourites and compare stores side by side
- Submit supermarket prices via receipt photos to keep data current
- Complete an in-app survey to help shape future development

### What Makes Us Different
- **First of its kind in Jersey** — no comparable local service exists
- **Free to use** — no subscription, no paywall, no login required
- **Community driven** — users submit receipts to keep prices updated
- **Mobile optimised** — works on any phone browser, no app store needed
- **Local focus** — built specifically for Jersey's unique supermarket landscape
- **Accessibility** — light/dark mode toggle for visually impaired users

## 3. The Market

### Target Users
Jersey residents who do a weekly grocery shop — estimated 40,000+ households. The platform is particularly valuable for:
- Families on a budget seeking to reduce weekly food spend
- Elderly residents on fixed incomes
- Young families and first-time renters
- Anyone who shops across multiple stores

### Market Opportunity
Jersey has no existing grocery price comparison tool. Supermarket pricing in Jersey is notoriously opaque — prices vary significantly between stores for identical products, with variances of up to £4.72 on a single item (Instant Coffee). JerseyBasket fills a genuine gap in the market.

### Traction
- **1,000+ active users** — achieved in under 4 weeks, zero marketing spend
- **Word of mouth growth** — no paid advertising to date
- **Community engagement** — active competition driving user-generated price data
- **Media potential** — Jersey Consumer Council already aware of the platform
